About Me

Kate received her BFA in Illustration from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte in the spring of 2016. Since completing her degree Kate has worked for local businesses to create unique illustrations and promotional material for social media platforms and print- most recently having an ad appear in the Charlotte Observer.

View her entire art portfolio via inkycactus.com

In August 2017 Kate recieved certification as a fullstack web developer through the completion of The Coding Bootcamp at UNCC. She currently resides in Charlotte, NC with her husband Adam, and American Eskimo pup Warhol.
